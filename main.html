Paris


Paris, the capital city of France, is famous for its beautiful architecture, rich history, romantic atmosphere, world-class museums, and delicious food. It is one of the most visited tourist destinations in the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A popular avenue filled with shops and cafés.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A relaxing way to explore the city.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery and flaky French pastry.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicious sweet treats.
  • French Onion Soup – A classic French dish.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes served with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Baguette – Traditional French bread enjoyed daily.
